Vehicle Selection

Consider factors like passenger capacity, luggage space, fuel efficiency, and intended use. Our vehicle categories are clearly labeled with these specifications, and our team can provide personalized recommendations based on your requirements.

Rental Policies

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a credit card for the security deposit. International customers need a valid passport and international driving permit.

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a fee of one day's rental charge.

Vehicle Features

Most of our vehicles include built-in GPS navigation. For vehicles without built-in GPS, we offer portable GPS devices for a small additional fee.

Yes, we provide various types of child seats (infant, toddler, booster) that meet safety standards. Please request these during booking.

Maintenance & Support

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. Simply call our emergency support number, and we'll arrange immediate help or a replacement vehicle if needed.

All vehicles undergo thorough maintenance checks between rentals and regular service intervals as per manufacturer specifications.
